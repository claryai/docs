## Upgrade Path

Clary AI is designed to grow with your needs. You can easily upgrade from one tier to another without losing any data or configurations. When you upgrade:

- All your existing documents and workflows are preserved
- New capabilities are immediately available
- No need to reconfigure your integrations
- Billing is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle

For more information on upgrading, see our [Upgrade Guide](/tiers/upgrade-guide).

## Choosing the Right Tier

<Accordion title="When to choose Lite">
  The Lite tier is ideal for:
  - Small businesses with basic document processing needs
  - Developers who want to integrate their own AI models
  - Testing and evaluation purposes
  - Budget-conscious users who need core document processing features

  **Use Case Example**: A small law firm that needs to extract text from contracts and organize them by type.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When to choose Standard">
  The Standard tier is perfect for:
  - Medium-sized businesses with regular document processing needs
  - Users who want AI-powered document analysis out of the box
  - Organizations that need more advanced workflow capabilities
  - Teams that require email support

  **Use Case Example**: An accounting department that processes invoices, extracts key data, and integrates with their accounting software.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When to choose Professional">
  The Professional tier is designed for:
  - Large enterprises with high-volume document processing
  - Organizations with complex document workflows
  - Users who need integration with cloud-based LLMs
  - Businesses that require priority support and SLAs

  **Use Case Example**: A healthcare provider processing patient records, insurance forms, and medical documents with strict compliance requirements.
</Accordion>
