# Upgrade Guide

This guide explains how to upgrade between Clary AI tiers and what to expect during the upgrade process.

## Upgrade Process Overview

Upgrading your Clary AI subscription is a straightforward process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Subscription Settings">
    Log in to your Clary AI account and navigate to Settings > Subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Tier">
    Click on "Upgrade" and select your desired tier (Standard or Professional).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes">
    Review the features and pricing of your new tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Upgrade">
    Confirm your upgrade. Your billing will be prorated for the remainder of your current billing cycle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="System Update">
    The system will automatically update to your new tier. This process typically takes a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens During an Upgrade

When you upgrade to a higher tier, the following occurs:

- **Immediate Access**: You gain immediate access to all features of your new tier.
- **Data Preservation**: All your existing documents, workflows, and configurations are preserved.
- **Prorated Billing**: Your billing is prorated for the remainder of your current billing cycle.
- **System Update**: The system automatically updates to include new capabilities.
- **Email Confirmation**: You receive an email confirmation of your upgrade.

## Downgrading

If you need to downgrade your subscription:

1. Navigate to Settings > Subscription
2. Click on "Downgrade" and select your desired tier
3. Review the changes and confirm your downgrade

<Warning>
  When downgrading, you'll lose access to features specific to your current tier. Your downgrade will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
</Warning>

### What to Expect When Downgrading

- **Feature Access**: You'll lose access to features specific to your current tier.
- **Data Preservation**: Your documents and basic workflows will be preserved.
- **Workflow Compatibility**: Complex workflows may need to be simplified to work with the lower tier.
- **Billing Cycle**: Your downgrade will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
